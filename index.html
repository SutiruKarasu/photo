About Me

Tatiana Lumos is a Prague-based photographer creating cinematic portraits and visual stories inspired by emotion, contrast, and inner transformation. Tatiana is my real name. Lumos is a pseudonym that, for me, became a symbol of light, intuition, and the quiet magic hidden within everyday life. I am inspired by contrasts in all their forms: softness and strength, closeness and distance, light and darkness, beauty and tension. I am drawn to images that hold emotion, ambiguity, and space for interpretation — photographs that invite you not only to look, but to feel.

Whether it's an intimate wedding, a cozy family session, or a creative editorial shoot, my goal is to make you feel comfortable and beautiful. Let's create timeless art together.
